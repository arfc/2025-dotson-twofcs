\opening{Dear Editors,}

Please find enclosed a revision of the manuscript entitled:
``\textit{Demonstrating the Osier framework for energy system and nuclear fuel
cycle optimization}'' which I and my coauthors, \textcolor{black}{Madicken Munk and
Kathryn D. Huff}, are submitting for exclusive consideration for publication
as a research article for the 2025 TWoFCS Special Issue in the Annals of Nuclear Energy.

This manuscript introduces the multi-objective optimization framework, \texttt{Osier},
and demonstrates how this tool may be used for analyzing nuclear fuel cycle options.
It also shows that \texttt{Osier} produces results consistent with more mature modeling
tools.

Thank you for your consideration of this work. I expect it will be of interest
to a broad readership concerned with the nuclear fuel cycle, advanced reactors,
energy system optimization, and applications of genetic algorithms in nuclear
engineering. Please address correspondence concerning this manuscript to me at
the University of Illinois.
